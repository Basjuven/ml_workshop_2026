# Introduction to Machine Learning 2026

A practical workshop for high school teachers to learn the fundamental concepts of **Machine Learning** and how to teach them in the classroom through simple and reproducible projects.

The goal is NOT to turn participants into expert programmers, but to help them become **facilitators** who can guide their students through data analysis and artificial intelligence projects.

---

## Workshop Objectives

By the end of the workshop, participants will be able to:

* Understand what Machine Learning is and what it is used for
* Explore real data with Python
* Visualize information to identify patterns
* Train a classification model
* Evaluate whether the model works correctly
* Adapt these activities for students
